## 🐤 Current project: CANARY

**CANARY** stands for **Component Analytics & Near-term Advisory Risk Yardstick**.

The idea is simple:

> Can we identify open-source components that may be at higher risk of a future security advisory **before** that advisory is published?

To explore that, I’m working on:
- collecting and normalizing historical OSS data
- building advisory-linked datasets
- experimenting with interpretable ML models
- comparing feature families to separate signal from noise
- designing a practical decision-support tool for supply-chain security
